Full Privacy Notice

Effective Date: February 17, 2026

At Crosspoint Church, the privacy and safety of every child in our care is of the utmost importance to us. This Privacy Notice explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, and how we protect it. By enrolling your child(ren) in Vacation Bible School (VBS), you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this notice.

1. Information We Collect

In order to provide a safe and well-organized program, we may collect the following information about your child(ren) and your family:

  • Child(ren)'s full name(s) and date(s) of birth
  • Parent or guardian name(s) and contact information
  • Emergency contact information
  • Relevant medical information or allergies as disclosed by the parent or guardian
  • Photo and/or video, only if permission has been granted on the accompanying consent form

2. How We Use Your Information

Personal information collected through VBS registration is used exclusively for program administration and approved communications. Specifically, your information is used to:

  • Register and identify your child(ren) during the program
  • Communicate program schedules, updates, and other essential information to parents and guardians
  • Respond to emergencies and ensure the health, safety, and well-being of your child(ren)
  • Accommodate medical needs or dietary restrictions as disclosed by parents or guardians
  • Notify you of future Crosspoint Church events or programs, only when you have provided explicit consent to receive such communications

3. We Do Not Share or Sell Your Information

Crosspoint Church does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information or your child(ren)'s personal information with any third party for commercial or marketing purposes. Your information is used exclusively for the administration of the VBS program and related church communications. We will never use your personal data for any purpose beyond what is described in this notice without first obtaining your explicit consent.

4. Payment Processing

VBS registration fees and other payments are processed securely through Stripe, a third-party payment processor. Crosspoint Church does not have access to, nor does it store, your complete credit card information. When you make a payment, your payment details are transmitted directly to Stripe's secure servers. For more information about how Stripe handles your payment information, please review Stripe's Privacy Policy.

5. Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

Crosspoint Church is deeply committed to protecting the privacy of children in our programs. We take the following steps to safeguard your child(ren)'s personal information:

  • Access to personal information is strictly limited to authorized staff and volunteers who require it to perform their responsibilities
  • Physical records are stored securely and are not left unattended or accessible to unauthorized individuals
  • Digital records, where applicable, are protected and access-controlled
  • Photo and video of children are only used when explicit permission has been granted by a parent or guardian

6. Your Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about your child(ren), to request corrections to any inaccurate information, or to request that your information be removed from our records following the conclusion of the program. To make such a request, please contact us using the information provided below.
